## Task 2 — Time Series Analysis

**Dataset:** AAPL Stock Prices (2014–2017)  
**Tool:** Python · pandas · matplotlib · statsmodels · Google Colab

### What was done:
- Loaded and parsed daily AAPL stock price data (2014–2017)
- Applied rolling mean to smooth short-term fluctuations
- Decomposed time series into trend, seasonality, and residual components
- Visualized price movement and identified upward long-term trend

### Key Findings:
- Clear **upward trend** in AAPL stock price across 2014–2017
- Seasonal patterns visible at regular intervals
- Rolling average (window=30) effectively removes daily noise
